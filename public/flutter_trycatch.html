The Navigation Stack

Visualizing Navigator.push and Navigator.pop behavior. The stack manages the history of routes.

MaterialPageRoute

When you call push, a new Route object is placed on top of the stack. The user sees the top-most route.

Navigator.pop

Removes the top route, revealing the one beneath it. If the stack becomes empty, the app exits.
