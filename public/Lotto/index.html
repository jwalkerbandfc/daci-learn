How to Use

👀 Viewing Draws

The calendar shows all lottery draws for the current month. Each day displays:

  • Purple badges = EuroMillions (always Fridays)
  • Blue badges = Lotto (always Saturdays)
  • Number showing how many people have registered interest

✋ Registering Interest

  1. Click the "Register Interest" button on any draw
  2. Enter your name and how many tickets you want (1 or 2)
  3. See payment instructions for Jason Walker
  4. Contact Jason via Teams, PayPal, or email to confirm payment
  5. Your name appears on the draw once registered

⚙️ Admin Panel (Password Protected)

Admins can:

  • Add new draws – Select type (EuroMillions/Lotto) and date
  • View registrations – See who's interested in each draw
  • Enter winning numbers – After the draw is done
  • Add results & notes – Document winners and payouts
  • Track withdrawals – Names of people who pulled out before 5pm
  • Delete draws – Remove incorrect or cancelled draws

📌 Important Notes

  • Withdrawals must be confirmed via Teams before 5pm on draw day
  • After 5pm, withdrawals are final
  • Payment instructions are shown when registering interest
  • Use the calendar arrows to view draws in other months
